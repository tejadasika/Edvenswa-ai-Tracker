What this extension records

  • The AI platform you're on (chatgpt, claude, gemini, …).
  • Foreground time on the page, in whole seconds.
  • Best-effort model name from the URL or page title.
  • Browser family (chrome / edge / firefox) and a random per-install ID.

What this extension never records

  • Your prompts, the AI's replies, or any DOM text.
  • Cookies, credentials, or page screenshots.
  • Activity on sites that aren't in the supported AI list.

Controls

  • Pause anytime from the toolbar popup.
  • Revoke the token from your dashboard to cut off all reporting.
